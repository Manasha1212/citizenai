About CitizenAI

CitizenAI is an intelligent citizen-engagement platform built to put actionable, expert-style guidance in the hands of everyday people. We help you understand your options and create ready-to-send documents — from tenant complaints to benefits appeals — without the legalese.

Why we exist: Access to practical, trustworthy guidance should not depend on money or insider knowledge. We bridge the gap between people and the systems that affect them.

Our promise: Fast, jargon-free, free-to-use tools that help you take confident action.

Start a Chat Explore Services

Our Mission

To democratize civic support: make clear, actionable help available to every person so they can resolve problems, assert their rights, and make informed choices.

Our Vision

A future where people don't need gatekeepers to access fair outcomes — where information, tools, and guidance are accessible and trusted by all.

Key Capabilities

What CitizenAI can do for you — powered by modern AI and built with civic trust in mind.

Chat Responses

Natural-language conversations to understand your issue and generate tailored documents, templates, and next steps.

Sentiment Analysis

Tone-aware responses: we detect urgency or frustration so replies are empathetic and appropriate.

Concern Reporting

Users can flag problems; reports are logged and routed for review or follow-up by community managers.

Dashboard Insights

Admins get analytics on trends, common issues, sentiment over time, and system performance to inform action.

AI & Engineering Stack

CitizenAI uses a mix of proven libraries and models to deliver reliable, efficient, and explainable results.

Core Model

IBM Granite — an enterprise-grade LLM tuned for instruction-style tasks and robust NLU.

Libraries & Tools

  • Flask — web framework for routing, templating, and session handling
  • PyTorch — model backend for efficient inference
  • transformers, accelerate, bitsandbytes — Hugging Face tooling for model loading, quantization, and multi-device handling

We also follow library docs closely around model loading, inference, quantization (8-bit/4-bit), and device placement to ensure smooth, cost-effective deployments.

Built with care by a small team

We're a compact cross-functional crew: product folks, civic lawyers (consulting), data scientists, and engineers. Our approach blends traditional civic knowledge with modern AI to produce grounded, useful outputs.

  • Human-in-the-loop review for high-stakes templates
  • Privacy-first data handling and opt-in telemetry
  • Transparent model disclaimers and citation when needed
